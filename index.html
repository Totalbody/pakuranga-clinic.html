About Our Clinic

Total Body Physio Pakuranga, established in 2002, has been proudly serving the community for over two decades. Our clinic is conveniently located within the Lloyd Elsmore Leisure Centre, connecting sports and physiotherapy in an accessible location. We offer a range of comprehensive physiotherapy services to cater to diverse needs, from sports injuries to chronic pain management.

Our state-of-the-art facility features spacious treatment rooms and access to top-quality pools and a well-equipped gym, providing an ideal environment for your rehabilitation journey. With ample parking and wheelchair-friendly access, we ensure a smooth experience for all our patients.
